Buddleja 'Black Knight'

Plant can live in full sun or light shade. Requires moderate to regular watering. Grows well in well-drained soil. Can grow to 12 feet tall and just as wide. Has willow like branches. Leaves grow 1-4 inches long, are dark green on top of leaf and gray and hairy underneath. Produces purple flowers in the spring. Prune after plant blooms


Colors: Purple/Green

Kind:Evergreen

Form:Shrubs

Characteristics:Fire Retardant, Shade Tolerant, Pink Flowering
